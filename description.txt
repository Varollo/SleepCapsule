This mod adds a Sleep Capsule you can make and right click to skip time.

==FEATURES==
- Skip to day or night depending on when you use it
- If it's raining, skip the rain by using it

==CRAFTING==
- At an Glass Kiln
- 4x Iron Bar
- 10x Glass

This mod is based on a mod by LIGION BOB.
I've decided to make my own version of the Sleep Mod without setting spawn everytime.
